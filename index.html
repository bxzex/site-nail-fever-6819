Information

Common Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to guarantee your preferred time and technician.

What is the difference between Gel and Dip Powder?

Gel polish is painted on and cured under a UV/LED light, offering a glossy, chip-free finish for up to 2 weeks. Dip powder involves dipping the nail into colored powder and sealing it, providing extra strength and durability lasting 3-4 weeks without UV light.

How do you sanitize your tools?

Your health and safety are our top priorities. We use hospital-grade autoclaves to sterilize all metal implements, and we use disposable liners, files, and buffers for every client.

Do you offer group bookings or parties?

Yes! We love hosting bridal parties, birthdays, and girls' days out. Please contact us directly by phone to arrange group accommodations and special requests.
